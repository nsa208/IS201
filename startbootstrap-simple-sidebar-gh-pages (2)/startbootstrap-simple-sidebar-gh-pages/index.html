About Me

I have a life rule. It goes, "if the opportunity to do anything outside of your comfort zone comes up,take it." This principle is reflected not only in my skiing style, but in my lifestyle. When I'm on the mountain, I take calculated risks. I'll ski a line faster than I have ever tried before, send a cliff bigger than I ever have, or explore a technical zone where falling means sliding for several hundred feet. I'm of the belief that it's important to take similarly calculated risks in life, for the same reason- there is almost no improvement inside the comfort zone. That is what drives me personally and professionally, the opportunity to explore.
